Fusion 360 Overview Page Hero Banner

A fullscreen hero banner to capture a page visitor's attention and offer an inspirational feeling. Multiple call to actions are present to encourage users to explore the product and its features. Fusion 360 Overview Page Feature Gallery

I created a tabbed interface "Feature Gallery" for the Fusion 360 overview page. Clicking on each tab reveals a different set of features, allowing users to explore the product's capabilities in a structured and engaging way. The gallery is designed to be responsive, ensuring that it looks great and functions well on all devices, from desktops to mobile phones.

Fusion 360 Product Tour

This is an elaborate "Product Tour" highlighting different capabilities of the Fusion 360 software package, including its extensions. The tour is designed to be interactive, allowing users to click through various features and learn more about each one. Each section has it's own lazy-loaded video to provide the user with a visual demonstration of the feature in action. This section provides a comprehensive overview of the product's functionalities, making it easier for potential users to understand its value. The design is responsive, ensuring that the tour is accessible and visually appealing on all devices.
